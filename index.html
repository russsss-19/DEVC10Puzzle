The Development Puzzle

Our artefact, titled “The Development Puzzle,” depicts how all elements of development need to unite in order to create a prosperous Philippines. Each puzzle piece symbolizes a vital component that helps the country grow. In the case of a real puzzle, development becomes complete only when all pieces are linked and appreciated equally. Real progress is done through inclusivity, unity, and by cooperation of the people. It serves as a reminder that every sector and individual plays an essential role in shaping a better future for all.
